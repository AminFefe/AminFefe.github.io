About Me

I currently work as a research scientist at Cairn Biosciences.

Before that, I graduated with a PhD in Image Processing and Computer Vision at the Center for Mathematical Morphology of Mines ParisTech, under the supervision of Fernand Meyer and Santiago Velasco-Forero.

I also hold a M.Sc. degree in Image Processing from Aix-Marseille University and a generalist engineer degree from Centrale Marseille in 2014.

My research interests include

  • Image Processing

  • Graph Signal Processing

  • Computer Vision

  • Pattern Recognition

  • Deep Learning
